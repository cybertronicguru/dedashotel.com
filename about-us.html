“Customers may forget what you said but they will never forget how you made them feel”.

De-Da's Hotels & Suites is the most preferred hotel in Port Harcourt and it's all about the right mix! Located in the heart of Port Harcourt and shielded from the hustle and bustle of the Rivers State metropolis, we offer our corporate clients and walk in guests a perfect blend of relaxation, activities, and Nigeria tradition delicately infused to meet the highest international standards.

Overlooking the De-Da's Hotel, it is just a 2-minute drive to Navy Pathfinder, And 3 minutes Drive To the City Centre 20 minutes to the Airport and Bori Camp Military Base.

Our hotels are designed for your comfort and convenience. Your security is our primary concern and you'll find our customer care second to none.

Our hotel has taken important steps to live up to West Africa's future model in the hospitality sector. With the best conference and banqueting facilities, tastefully furnished to a 700-seater capacity, a world-class swimming pool, 45 excellently furnished rooms spread across mostly with a choice of city and sea views.

De-Da's is equipped with a state of the art health and fitness centre comprising of a gym, a tennis/basket ball court, a volley ball court, a sauna, spa, salon, and nail studio. We have an in-house medical clinic as well.

We are passionate about serving you and look forward to having you.
